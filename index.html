We are a group of creative hopeful students

who saw that Egypt suffers from the lack of scientific interests and facilities that provide services for innovative youth. So, we eagerly in need for making a new generation who can change our community into a better one. In order to do this and develop our country especially delayed Upper Egypt we created this team. The team was established in Sohag, April 2019 was the start and the stand of our team.

Our Vision

We are all aware that Upper Egypt suffers from delays. So, We in the name of “Benovate” aim to increase the level of interests in new careers for youth communities, we want all youth to start applying the idea of Benovate, which is firstly, believing in yourself whatever how old are you also wherever you are and the second thing is innovating and applying creativity in various field to develop our way of thinking.

Our Mission

The team has a lot of difficult tasks and work to reach its progress, so it will need a lot of efforts and time to achieve the goal of the vision of the team by inspiring our community to apply our Slogan “Beyond the limits”, where nothing is impossible. So, the team will organize a lot of events, trends competitions and initiatives that work on achieving our goal and share knowledge and hope allover Upper Egypt.
